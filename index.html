Privacy Policy for Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

At Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our iOS application. By accessing or using Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life, you consent to the practices described below.

Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life is a personal journey and goal tracking app designed to help you rebuild from challenging moments in life. We believe in transparency and are committed to safeguarding your sensitive personal reflections.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Authentication: When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique anonymous identifier. Apple does not share your email address or name with us unless you explicitly choose to share it.
  • Personal Reflections: During onboarding, you provide written descriptions of your current challenges, goals, and areas you want to improve. This text is used solely to generate your personalized improvement plan.
  • Payment Information: Subscription payments are processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system and Superwall. We do not have access to or store your credit card details, billing address, or other payment information.

1.2 Journey Data

  • Journey Start Date: The date you began your journey, used to calculate progress milestones and unlock badges.
  • Milestone Progress: Records of which achievement badges you've unlocked based on time elapsed since your journey start.
  • Improvement Plan: The AI-generated personalized plan created from your initial reflections.

1.3 Device Information

  • Device Type: iOS version and device model for app compatibility purposes.
  • App Version: The version of Rock Bottom you're using to ensure proper functionality.
  • Device Identifier: A unique device identifier used for analytics purposes (see Section 3.4).

1.4 Usage Data

  • Product Interactions: We collect anonymized data about how you interact with the app, including screens viewed, buttons tapped, and navigation patterns. This helps us improve the user experience.
  • Session Recordings: We use session replay technology to record anonymized sessions of app usage. See Section 3.4 for details on how we protect your privacy in these recordings.
What We DO NOT Collect:
  • We do NOT collect IP addresses
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT collect contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT use data for advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Generate Your Personalized Plan: Your initial reflections are sent to OpenAI's GPT-4 API to create a customized improvement roadmap tailored to your specific situation.
  • Track Your Journey Progress: Calculate days since your journey start to unlock milestone badges and visualize your progress.
  • Manage Your Subscription: Verify your subscription status to provide access to premium features through Apple's In-App Purchase system.
  • Display Your Plan: Store and display your personalized improvement plan for ongoing reference.
  • Sync Across Devices (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync, your journey data syncs across your Apple devices for seamless access.
  • Improve the App: Analyze anonymized usage patterns and session recordings to identify issues, improve features, and enhance the overall user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we work with the following trusted service providers to deliver core app functionality:

3.1 OpenAI

Your onboarding reflections are sent securely to OpenAI's API to generate your personalized improvement plan. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy and data usage policies:

  • What's Shared: Only the text you provide during onboarding describing your challenges and goals.
  • Purpose: To generate your personalized improvement plan using GPT-4.
  • OpenAI's Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Data Retention: OpenAI may retain API requests for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, then deletes them. They do not use your data to train their models.

3.2 Apple Services

  • Sign in with Apple: Provides secure authentication without sharing your personal information with us. Learn more: Apple's Sign in with Apple Privacy
  • In-App Purchases: All subscription payments are processed by Apple. We receive only confirmation of your subscription status.
  • iCloud (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync, your journey data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account, not on our servers.

3.3 Superwall

Superwall provides our paywall and subscription management interface. They process:

  • Anonymous device identifiers
  • Subscription status and events
  • Paywall interaction data (e.g., which subscription tier you viewed)

Learn more: Superwall Privacy Policy

3.4 Mixpanel (Analytics & Session Replay)

We use Mixpanel to understand how users interact with Rock Bottom and to improve the app experience. Mixpanel provides:

  • Analytics: Anonymized data about app usage, including which features are used most, where users encounter issues, and how users navigate through the app.
  • Session Replay: Recordings of user sessions that show screen content and interactions (taps, scrolls, navigation) to help us identify usability issues and improve the experience.
Session Replay Privacy Protections:
  • Sensitive content is automatically masked: Text you enter about your personal challenges, your name, and your age are never recorded in session replays. These fields appear as black rectangles in recordings.
  • Text inputs are always hidden: Any text you type into input fields is automatically masked by the SDK.
  • No personal data in recordings: Session replays capture only anonymized interaction patterns, not your personal information.
  • Used solely for product improvement: Session recordings are not shared with third parties and are used only to improve the Rock Bottom experience.

Learn more: Mixpanel Privacy Policy

3.5 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information when required to:

  • Comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • Protect the rights, safety, and property of Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life, our users, or others
  • Investigate and prevent fraud, security issues, or technical problems
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions

3.6 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice in the app before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Lives

Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life stores most app data locally on your device. We do not operate servers or databases that store your personal reflections or improvement plans. Specifically:

  • Local Storage: Journey data, improvement plan, and preferences are stored in your device's secure storage using iOS UserDefaults and app containers.
  • iCloud (Optional): If enabled, data syncs to your personal iCloud account using Apple's encrypted CloudKit services.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized usage data and session recordings are stored on Mixpanel's secure servers.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to OpenAI and Mixpanel is sent over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • iOS Security: Your local data benefits from iOS's built-in security features, including app sandboxing and device encryption.
  • Sign in with Apple: Provides two-factor authentication and privacy protections.
  • No Passwords: We never store passwords because authentication is handled entirely by Apple.
  • Sensitive Data Masking: Session replay automatically masks sensitive fields to prevent recording of personal information.

Important Security Note: While we implement strong security practices, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to protect your data using industry best practices.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location (including GDPR in the EU, CCPA in California, and other privacy laws), you may have the following rights regarding your data:

5.1 Access and Portability

  • Access Your Data: All your data is stored locally on your device and is always accessible to you through the app interface.
  • Export Your Data: Contact us at the email below to request a copy of any data processed through our third-party services (e.g., OpenAI API requests, Mixpanel analytics).

  • Correct Inaccuracies: Contact us if you believe any data processed on your behalf is inaccurate.

  • Delete iCloud Data: Disable iCloud sync and delete the app to remove synced data from iCloud.

5.4 Restriction and Objection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at team@pathapp.net.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Local Data

Data stored on your device remains until you:

  • Delete the Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life app
  • Reset your journey through the app settings
  • Manually clear app data through iOS Settings → Rock Bottom - Reset Your Life → Reset App

6.2 Third-Party Data

  • Apple: Subscription records are retained according to Apple's policies for tax and legal compliance.
  • Superwall: Subscription events are retained according to their data retention policy.
  • Mixpanel: Analytics data and session recordings are retained according to Mixpanel's data retention policies and our account settings.

6.3 Legal Retention

We may retain certain data longer if required by law, such as transaction records for tax purposes or to comply with legal investigations.

7.1 What We Track

  • Which screens you visit and how you navigate the app
  • Which features you use most frequently
  • Where you might encounter errors or confusion

7.2 What We DON'T Track

7.3 Session Replay

Session replay creates anonymized recordings of how you interact with the app. These recordings help us:

Your privacy is protected: Sensitive information (your personal challenges, name, and age) is automatically masked and appears as black rectangles in recordings. We never see what you type in these fields.
